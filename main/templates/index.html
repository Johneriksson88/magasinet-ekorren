About us

Magasinet Ekorren opened in the autumn of 2020. Located only 10 minutes by car from the Uppsala city centre it provides highly available storage solutions without the higher renting costs of more centrally located facilities.

We at Magasinet Ekorren believe that things should be as easy as possible. Our customers can rent a storage unit, sign a contract and move in on the same day. We understand that different life events call for different solutions, and we will go all the way in trying to accommodate any changes or modifications to make your experience with us as flawless as possible. We have multiple different solutions that we can sow into a personal package for you.

Our storage units are available all days of the week, and using them is completely contact-less if you so wish. You get a personal code, you can drive up to the gate, unload, and drive on without having the hassle of having to reverse thanks to how we laid out our roads. Different carts are provided so you don't have to break your back.

We also provide archiving solutions for e.g. accounting firms, with archives up to date with all legal requirements.
